Section 1:
HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
HOUSE BILL 4107
By COMMITTEE ON HEALTH CARE
February 16
1 On page 1 of the printed bill, delete lines 18 through 24 and insert:
2 “(d) ‘Specialty urgent care center’ means a facility or portion of a facility that provides to in-
3 dividuals, on a walk-in and episodic basis:
4 “(A) For illnesses or injuries that are acute but not life-threatening or that do not require
5 emergency medical services, medical care related only to a specific body system; or
6 “(B) Behavioral health care services only.
7 “(e)(A) ‘Urgent care center’ means a facility or portion of a facility that:
8 “(i) Uses the term ‘urgent’ or ‘urgent care’ to describe the facility or portion of the facility; and
9 “(ii) Provides medical care to individuals only on a walk-in and episodic basis for illnesses or
10 injuries that are acute but not life-threatening or that do not require emergency medical
11 services.”.
12 On page 3, delete line 11 and insert “an emergency department of a hospital licensed under ORS
13 441.025.”.
14
