House Bill 4107
Ordered by the House February 16
Including House Amendments dated February 16
Sponsored by Representative NATHANSON, Senator PROZANSKI; Representatives ANDERSEN, BOWMAN,
FRAGALA, JAVADI, MCDONALD, MUNOZ, WALTERS, Senator PATTERSON (Presession filed.)
SUMMARY
The following summary is not prepared by the sponsors of the measure and is not a part of the body thereof subject
to consideration by the Legislative Assembly. It is an editor’s brief statement of the essential features of the
measure. The statement includes a measure digest written in compliance with applicable readability standards.
Digest: The Act says an urgent care center has to make some information public and has to
offer certain health care services. (Flesch Readability Score: 64.6).
Requires an urgent care center to make publicly available specified information about the ur-
gent care center, offer specified services and, except in certain circumstances, have at least one li-
censed health care provider on site during the hours of operation. Defines “urgent care center.”
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT
2 Relating to urgent care centers.
3 Be It Enacted by the People of the State of Oregon:
4 SECTION 1. (1) As used in this section:
5 (a) “Health care facility” has the meaning given that term in ORS 442.015.
6 (b) “Health system” means:
7 (A) A parent corporation of one or more health care facilities, urgent care centers or
8 other entities that provide health care services, and any entity affiliated with the parent
9 corporation through ownership, governance, control or membership; or
10 (B) A health care facility, urgent care center or other entity that provides health care
11 services, and any entity affiliated with the health care facility, urgent care center or other
12 entity that provides health care services through ownership, governance, control or mem-
13 bership.
14 (c) “Licensed health care provider” means:
15 (A) A physician licensed under ORS chapter 677;
16 (B) A physician associate licensed under ORS 677.505 to 677.525; or
17 (C) A nurse practitioner licensed under ORS 678.375 to 678.390.
18 (d) “Specialty urgent care center” means a facility or portion of a facility that provides
19 to individuals, on a walk-in and episodic basis:
20 (A) For illnesses or injuries that are acute but not life-threatening or that do not require
21 emergency medical services, medical care related only to a specific body system; or
22 (B) Behavioral health care services only.
23 (e)(A) “Urgent care center” means a facility or portion of a facility that:
24 (i) Uses the term “urgent” or “urgent care” to describe the facility or portion of the fa-
25 cility; and
26 (ii) Provides medical care to individuals only on a walk-in and episodic basis for illnesses
