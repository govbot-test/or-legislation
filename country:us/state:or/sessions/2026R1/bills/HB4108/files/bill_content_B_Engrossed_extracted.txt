Allows the City of Eugene to annex noncontiguous land upon receipt of a petition from all

1 A BILL FOR AN ACT
2 Relating to annexation of land noncontiguous to a city.
3 Be It Enacted by the People of the State of Oregon:
4 SECTION 1. (1) Notwithstanding any contrary provision of a city charter or ordinance,
5 upon receipt of a petition proposing annexation of noncontiguous land submitted by all own-
6 ers of the land, the legislative body of the City of Eugene may annex the land without holding
7 a public hearing or submitting the proposal to the electors of the city if:
8 (a) The land is located within the city’s acknowledged urban growth boundary.
9 (b) The land is designated for residential or mixed use on the city’s acknowledged com-
10 prehensive plan, excluding land designated for heavy industrial uses.
11 (c) The land is connected to and receives water, wastewater and stormwater services
12 from the city, a unit of local government as defined in ORS 190.003, or a contracted operator
13 of the water system or primary water supplier for the land.
14 (d) The land is accessible by public road.
15 (2) Upon determining that the noncontiguous land proposed for annexation satisfies the
16 criteria described under subsection (1) of this section, the legislative body of the city may,
17 by resolution or ordinance, declare that the land is annexed to the city and set the final
18 boundaries of the area to be annexed by a legal description.
