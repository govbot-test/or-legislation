Section 1:
HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
A-ENGROSSED HOUSE BILL 4100
By JOINT COMMITTEE ON WAYS AND MEANS
March 4
1 On page 6 of the printed A-engrossed bill, after line 2, insert:
2 “SECTION 11. Notwithstanding any other law limiting expenditures, the limitation on
3 expenditures established by section 2 (3), chapter 426, Oregon Laws 2025, for the biennium
4 ending June 30, 2027, as the maximum limit for payment of expenses from fees, moneys or
5 other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, the proceeds of bonds for the Orphan Site
6 Account and federal funds from congestion mitigation and air quality grants, drinking water
7 protection, laboratory accreditation and woodstove grants and for smoke monitoring labora-
8 tory services, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds not described in section 2, chap-
9 ter 426, Oregon Laws 2025, collected or received by the Department of Environmental
10 Quality, for land quality, is increased by $175,162, to carry out sections 2 to 8 of this 2026
11 Act and the amendments to ORS 468B.513 and 468B.525 by sections 9 and 10 of this 2026
12 Act.”.
13 In line 3, delete “11” and insert “12”.
14
