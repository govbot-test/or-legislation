83rd OREGON LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY--2026 Regular Session
House Bill 4174
Sponsored by COMMITTEE ON RULES (at the request of Representative Ben Bowman)
SUMMARY
The following summary is not prepared by the sponsors of the measure and is not a part of the body thereof subject
to consideration by the Legislative Assembly. It is an editor’s brief statement of the essential features of the
measure as introduced. The statement includes a measure digest written in compliance with applicable readability
standards.
Digest: Tells DOJ to study and report on public records. (Flesch Readability Score: 75.5).
Requires the Department of Justice to study public records. Directs the department to submit
findings to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to the judiciary no later than
September 15, 2027.
Sunsets on January 2, 2028.
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT
2 Relating to public records.
3 Be It Enacted by the People of the State of Oregon:
4 SECTION 1. The Department of Justice shall study public records. The department shall
5 submit a report in the manner provided by ORS 192.245, and may include recommendations
6 for legislation, to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to the judiciary
7 no later than September 15, 2027.
8 SECTION 2. Section 1 of this 2026 Act is repealed on January 2, 2028.
