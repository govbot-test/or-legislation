Section 1:
SB 1597-1
(LC 260)
2/9/26 (VSR/DJ/ps)
Requested by Senator FREDERICK
PROPOSED AMENDMENTS TO
1 Delete lines 5 through 15 of the printed bill and insert:
2 “SECTION 2. (1) As used in this section:
3 “(a) ‘Electric utility’ means a public utility, a people’s utility dis-
4 trict organized under ORS chapter 261, a municipal utility operating
5 under ORS chapter 225 or a cooperative organized under ORS chapter
6 62 that provides or delivers electricity service.
7 “(b) ‘Energy facility’ means a nuclear energy facility located in
8 Oregon that commences generating electricity on or after the effective
9 date of this 2026 Act.
10 “(2) An electric utility shall disclose to its customers the costs of
11 storing nuclear waste produced by an energy facility in generating
12 electricity that the electric utility recovers in the rates of the electric
13 utility.
14 “(3) Prior to developing the energy facility, a person who seeks to
15 develop an energy facility to generate electricity that is to be sold by
16 an electric utility shall disclose to the electric utility and the public
17 the costs of storing nuclear waste that will be produced by the energy
18 facility in generating electricity.”.
19
