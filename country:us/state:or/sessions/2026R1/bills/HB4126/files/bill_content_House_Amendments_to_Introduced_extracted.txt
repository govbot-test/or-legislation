Section 1:
HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
HOUSE BILL 4126
By COMMITTEE ON TRANSPORTATION
February 18
1 Delete lines 14 and 15 of the printed bill and insert:
2 “(b) An estimate of the total vehicle miles driven on the public highways of this state based on
3 the most recently available data on vehicle miles traveled, set forth separately according to the
4 following weight table:
5 “(A) Vehicles weighing 10,000 pounds or less.
6 “(B) Vehicles weighing more than 10,000 pounds and not more than 12,000 pounds.
7 “(C) Vehicles weighing more than 12,000 pounds and not more than 14,000 pounds.
8 “(D) Vehicles weighing more than 14,000 pounds and not more than 16,000 pounds.
9 “(E) Vehicles weighing more than 16,000 pounds and not more than 18,000 pounds.
10 “(F) Vehicles weighing more than 18,000 pounds and not more than 20,000 pounds.
11 “(G) Vehicles weighing more than 20,000 pounds and not more than 22,000 pounds.
12 “(H) Vehicles weighing more than 22,000 pounds and not more than 24,000 pounds.
13 “(I) Vehicles weighing more than 24,000 pounds and not more than 26,000 pounds.
14 “(J) Vehicles weighing more than 26,000 pounds.”.
15 Delete lines 24 through 26 and insert:
16 “SECTION 3. Notwithstanding the due date for submission of reports under section 2 (1)
17 of this 2026 Act, the Department of Transportation shall submit the first report that is due
18 after the effective date of this 2026 Act not later than January 31, 2027.
19 “SECTION 4. This 2026 Act being necessary for the immediate preservation of the public
20 peace, health and safety, an emergency is declared to exist, and this 2026 Act takes effect
21 on its passage.”.
