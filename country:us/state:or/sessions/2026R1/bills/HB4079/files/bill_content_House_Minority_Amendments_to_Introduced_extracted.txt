HOUSE MINORITY REPORT
AMENDMENTS TO
HOUSE BILL 4079
By Nonconcurring Members of COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION
February 13
1 On page 2 of the printed bill, line 27, delete “and”.
2 In line 29, delete the period and insert “; and
3 “(D) The requirement to cooperate with federal immigration authorities as provided by section
4 6 of this 2026 Act when a person charged with or convicted of certain crimes has entered school
5 property.”.
6 On page 4, line 35, delete “and”.
7 In line 37, delete the period and insert “; and
8 “(D) The requirement to cooperate with federal immigration authorities as provided by section
9 6 of this 2026 Act when a person charged with or convicted of certain crimes has entered a
10 campus.”.
11 On page 5, after line 15, insert:
12 “SECTION 6. Notwithstanding any other provision of Oregon law, all state and local law
13 enforcement agencies and all employees and officers of a school district, as defined in section
14 1 of this 2026 Act, or an institution of higher education, as defined in section 4 of this 2026
15 Act, shall cooperate with federal immigration authorities, as defined in ORS 180.805, in car-
16 rying out any action concerning any person who:
17 “(1) Is charged with, or convicted of, a crime constituting a felony or a Class A
18 misdemeanor under Oregon law; and
19 “(2) Has entered school property, as defined in section 1 of this 2026 Act, or a campus,
20 as defined in section 4 of this 2026 Act.”.
21 In line 16, delete “6” and insert “7”.
22
