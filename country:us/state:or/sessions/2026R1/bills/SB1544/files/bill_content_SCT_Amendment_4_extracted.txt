13 porated city.

18 operate a motor vehicle dismantling business;

7 the Department of Transportation may issue a supplemental dismantler cer-

9 “(a) Shall consider the extent of development of surrounding property as
10 a residential area;

13 “(c) Shall consider the sufficiency in number of other motor vehicle dis-
