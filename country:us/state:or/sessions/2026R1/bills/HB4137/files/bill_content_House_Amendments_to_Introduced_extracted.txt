Section 1:
HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
HOUSE BILL 4137
By COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY
February 18
1 On page 2 of the printed bill, delete lines 26 through 40 and insert:
2 “(8)(a) A defendant who has been issued a violation citation for a traffic violation and who has
3 already transferred the defendant’s interest in the motor vehicle described in the citation is not re-
4 quired to make a first appearance if, within 30 days of the issuance of the citation, the defendant
5 instead notifies the court with jurisdiction, through a certificate of innocence or in the manner
6 provided by the court, of the transfer when the defendant submits to the court a statement swearing
7 or affirming that:
8 “(A) The citation is issued for the parking, abandoning or operation of the motor vehicle by
9 another person;
10 “(B) The defendant transferred interest in the vehicle to another person before the citation was
11 issued;
12 “(C) The defendant submitted the notice of transfer of interest to the Department of Transpor-
13 tation as required under ORS 803.112; and
14 “(D) Pursuant to ORS 803.117 the defendant is exempt from liability.
15 “(b) Upon determining that the person is exempt from liability in accordance with ORS 803.117,
16 the court shall dismiss the citation.”.
17
LC 251/HB 4137-2
