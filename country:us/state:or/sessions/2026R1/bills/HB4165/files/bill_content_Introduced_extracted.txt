83rd OREGON LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY--2026 Regular Session
House Bill 4165
Sponsored by COMMITTEE ON RULES (at the request of Representative Ben Bowman)
SUMMARY
The following summary is not prepared by the sponsors of the measure and is not a part of the body thereof subject
to consideration by the Legislative Assembly. It is an editor’s brief statement of the essential features of the
measure as introduced. The statement includes a measure digest written in compliance with applicable readability
standards.
Digest: The Act tells the SOS to study campaign finance. (Flesch Readability Score: 84.9).
Requires the Secretary of State to study campaign finance. Directs the Secretary of State to
submit findings to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to elections no later
than September 15, 2027.
Sunsets on January 2, 2028.
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT
2 Relating to campaign finance.
3 Be It Enacted by the People of the State of Oregon:
4 SECTION 1. The Secretary of State shall study campaign finance. The Secretary of State
5 shall submit a report in the manner provided by ORS 192.245, and may include recommen-
6 dations for legislation, to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to
7 elections no later than September 15, 2027.
8 SECTION 2. Section 1 of this 2026 Act is repealed on January 2, 2028.
