Section 1:
HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
HOUSE BILL 4069
By COMMITTEE ON BEHAVIORAL HEALTH
February 12
1 On page 1 of the printed bill, delete lines 18 through 30.
2 On page 2, delete lines 1 through 3 and insert:
3 “(c) ‘Lone worker’ means an individual who directly provides or assists with the delivery of
4 behavioral health services and works in a situation or location without a colleague nearby or
5 without close or direct supervision.
6 “(2) A behavioral health employer shall:
7 “(a) Implement a written policy or plan for the physical safety of individuals working for the
8 behavioral health employer.
9 “(b) Provide a copy of the safety policy or plan to new workers upon hire.
10 “(3) A safety policy or plan required under this section must:
11 “(a) Include a plan for addressing the physical safety of lone workers.
12 “(b) Include a plan for the safety of the built environment, including:
13 “(A) How workers may report structural security hazards; and
14 “(B) The time frame within which the behavioral health employer must respond to a report of
15 a structural security hazard.
16 “(c) Include information for workers about the safety training that the behavioral health em-
17 ployer is required to provide under ORS 430.775, including the time frame within which the employer
18 must provide all required training for a new worker and how often the employer must provide re-
19 newal training for current workers.
20 “(d) Be tailored to the behavioral health employer’s specific context.
21 “(e) Be easily accessible to staff.”.
