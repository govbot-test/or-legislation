Section 1:
SENATE AMENDMENTS TO
SENATE BILL 1523
By COMMITTEE ON HOUSING AND DEVELOPMENT
February 13
1 On page 1 of the printed bill, delete lines 7 through 25.
2 On page 2, delete lines 1 through 7 and insert:
3 “SECTION 2. (1) A landlord who uses a tenant portal to accept an application shall:
4 “(a)(A) Post a printable copy of the application on the landlord’s website; or
5 “(B) Within seven days following receipt of a written request by any prospective appli-
6 cant, provide a printed or printable copy of the application to the prospective applicant by
7 mail or electronic mail.
8 “(b) Process all applications whether received through the tenant portal or outside of the
9 portal.
10 “(2) If an applicant or tenant requests an alternative in writing, a landlord may not re-
11 quire a tenant to use a tenant portal as the sole means to:
12 “(a) Verify identification;
13 “(b) Review and sign addenda or other legal agreements; or
14 “(c) Submit documents relating to the tenancy.
15 “(3) This section does not prohibit a landlord from entering tenant information into a
16 tenant portal or otherwise using a tenant portal.
17 “(4) If a landlord fails to comply with this section, an individual who applied as described
18 in subsection (1)(b) of this section or requested an alternative to the tenant portal under
19 subsection (1)(a) or (2) of this section is entitled to any damages sustained, or $100, which-
20 ever is greater.”.
21
LC 287/SB 1523-4
