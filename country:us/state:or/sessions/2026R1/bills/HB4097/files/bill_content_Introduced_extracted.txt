Digest: This Act lets the Oregon Ocean Science Trust form a nonprofit. (Flesch Readability
Score: 72.6).
Authorizes the Oregon Ocean Science Trust to create and maintain a nonprofit organization.
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT
2 Relating to the Oregon Ocean Science Trust.
3 Be It Enacted by the People of the State of Oregon:
4 SECTION 1. Pursuant to ORS 196.566, the Oregon Ocean Science Trust may create and
5 maintain an entity that is exempt from federal income tax under section 501(c)(3) of the
6 Internal Revenue Code, as amended, for the purpose of advancing the mission of the trust.
