83rd OREGON LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY--2026 Regular Session
HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
HOUSE BILL 4021
By COMMITTEE ON RULES
February 18
1 On page 1 of the printed bill, after line 2, insert:
2 “Whereas state agencies routinely adopt administrative rules that impose new compliance obli-
3 gations or expose regulated entities to penalties for noncompliance; and
4 “Whereas under current law, most administrative rules become effective immediately upon filing
5 with the Secretary of State unless a later effective date is specified; and
6 “Whereas the immediate effectiveness of new rules may not provide regulated entities sufficient
7 time to understand, implement and comply with new requirements after they have been finalized;
8 now, therefore,”.
9 Delete lines 6 through 25.
10 On page 2, delete lines 1 and 2 and insert:
11 “SECTION 2. (1) This section applies to the following agencies:
12 “(a) The Department of Environmental Quality.
13 “(b) The Department of Transportation.
14 “(c) The Department of State Lands.
15 “(d) The State Department of Geology and Mineral Industries.
16 “(2) Notwithstanding ORS 183.355 (3), a permanent rule adopted, amended or repealed by
17 an agency listed in subsection (1) of this section that creates or modifies compliance re-
18 quirements for persons regulated by the agency may take effect no earlier than 28 days after
19 the date the final rule is filed with the Secretary of State as provided in ORS 183.355.
20 “(3) Notwithstanding ORS 183.335 (1), prior to the adoption, amendment or repeal of a
21 permanent rule, an agency listed in subsection (1) of this section shall give notice of its in-
22 tended action:
23 “(a) In the manner established by rule adopted by the agency under ORS 183.341 (4),
24 which provides a reasonable opportunity for interested persons to be notified of the agency’s
25 proposed action;
26 “(b) In the bulletin referred to in ORS 183.360 at least 21 days prior to the date the rule
27 is filed with the Secretary of State as provided in ORS 183.355;
28 “(c) At least 28 days before the date the rule is filed with the Secretary of State as pro-
29 vided in ORS 183.355, to persons who have requested notice pursuant to ORS 183.335 (8); and
30 “(d) Delivered only by email, at least 49 days before the date the rule is filed with the
31 Secretary of State as provided in ORS 183.355, to the persons specified in ORS 183.335 (15).
32 “(4) This section does not apply to adoption, amendment or suspension of a rule under
33 ORS 183.335 (5).”.
34 Delete line 4 and insert “information for questions about each rule adopted,”.
