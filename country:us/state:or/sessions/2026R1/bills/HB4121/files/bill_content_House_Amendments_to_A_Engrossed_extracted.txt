20 expertise in emergency management. The Governor shall seek the advice of the Oregon
21 Homeland Security Council when making appointments to the authority. Members of the
22 authority serve at the pleasure of the Governor.
23 “(3) If the Governor declares a state of emergency under ORS 401.165, the Governor may
24 activate the authority. The authority remains activated until deactivated by order of the
25 Governor. The authority may remain activated notwithstanding the termination of a state
26 of emergency.

28 “(a) Coordinate initial crisis services;

31 Agency; and
32 “(c) Coordinate the provision of emergency funding and other aid to local governments
33 or private entities.

35 of business.
36 “(6) Official action by the authority requires the approval of a majority of the members
37 of the authority.

39 person.
40 “(8) The authority shall meet at times and places specified by the call of the Governor
41 or the chairperson. The authority may meet at times when the authority is not activated to
42 maintain readiness in the event of an emergency.
43 “(9) The authority may adopt rules necessary for the operation of the authority.
44 “(10) The Office of the Governor shall provide staff support to the authority.
45 “(11) Members of the authority are not entitled to compensation or reimbursement for
