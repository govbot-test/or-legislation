Section 1:
HB 4007-13
(LC 255)
2/4/26 (HE/ps)
Requested by Representative MCLAIN
PROPOSED AMENDMENTS TO
1 On page 2 of the printed bill, delete lines 23 through 27 and insert:
2 “SECTION 4. Local government and state agency regulation of the
3 operation of powered micromobility devices. Local governments, as
4 defined in ORS 174.116, and state agencies having jurisdiction over bi-
5 cycle lanes, bicycle paths, sidewalks and trails of public passage may:
6 “(1) Prohibit the operation of powered micromobility devices on bi-
7 cycle lanes, bicycle paths, sidewalks and trails; or
8 “(2) Regulate, by ordinance or rule and by traffic control device, the
9 operation of powered micromobility devices on bicycle lanes, bicycle
10 paths, sidewalks and trails and the time, place and manner of the op-
11 eration of powered micromobility devices on bicycle lanes, bicycle
12 paths, sidewalks and trails.”.
13 On page 18, line 34, delete “26” and insert “26a”.
14
