13 “(a) For a city with a population of 25,000 or greater, may not exceed 100

18 shall determine whether the site would substantially comply with the appli-
19 cable provisions of sections 49 to 59, chapter 110, Oregon Laws 2024.

21 Metro shall:

1 “(A) Notify the city of deficiencies in the petition, specifying sufficient

3 and
4 “(B) Allow the city to amend its conceptual plan and resubmit it as a
5 petition to Metro under this section.
6 “(c) If Metro determines that a petition does comply, notwithstanding any
7 other provision of ORS chapter 197A, Metro shall adopt amendments to its
8 urban growth boundary to include the site in the petition, unless the
9 amendment would result in more than 300 total net [residential] buildable
10 acres added under this subsection.
11 “(d) Metro may not conduct a hearing to review or select petitions or
12 adopt amendments to its urban growth boundary under this section.
13 “(e) Sites added to the Metro urban growth boundary under this section
14 do not affect the maximum acreage of sites that may be added under section
15 51 (3) or (4), chapter 110, Oregon Laws 2024.
16 “(5) A city that adds or petitions to add a site under this section:
17 “(a) Is not required to demonstrate need for housing lands or affordable

20 section 50 (1) or 56, chapter 110, Oregon Laws 2024, or section 2, chapter 341,

22 110, Oregon Laws 2024.
