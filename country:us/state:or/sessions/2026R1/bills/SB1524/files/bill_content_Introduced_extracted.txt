83rd OREGON LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY--2026 Regular Session
Senate Bill 1524
Sponsored by Senator HAYDEN (Presession filed.)
SUMMARY
The following summary is not prepared by the sponsors of the measure and is not a part of the body thereof subject
to consideration by the Legislative Assembly. It is an editor’s brief statement of the essential features of the
measure as introduced. The statement includes a measure digest written in compliance with applicable readability
standards.
Digest: The Act uses money from the lottery to support horse racing. (Flesch Readability Score:
64.9).
Creates a continuing allocation of lottery revenues to support specified horse racing events and
facilities. Becomes operative July 1, 2027.
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT
2 Relating to horse racing; creating new provisions; and amending ORS 565.446 and 565.447.

6 SECTION 3. (1) The Horse Racing Account is established in the State Treasury, separate
7 and distinct from the General Fund. The account consists of moneys allocated under ORS
8 565.447 and all other moneys deposited in or transferred to the account by the Legislative
9 Assembly or otherwise. Interest earned by the account shall be credited to the account. The
10 moneys in the account are continuously appropriated to the Oregon Racing Commission to
11 be used as provided in subsection (2) of this section.
12 (2) The commission shall use the moneys in the account to support horse racing events
13 operated by licensees described in ORS 462.057 (2), and horse racing events held on county
14 fairgrounds in Crook County, Josephine County, Tillamook County and Union County, by
15 carrying out any of the following activities:
16 (a) Maintaining horse racing tracks at the fairgrounds;
17 (b) Supporting purse money and jockey incentives to attract better horse and human
18 athletes;
19 (c) Funding veterinarians to treat horses at the horse racing tracks and medical profes-
20 sionals to treat jockeys at the horse racing tracks;
21 (d) Purchasing horse racing safety equipment for use at the horse racing events;
22 (e) Operating the horse racing events, including paying for the costs of security and in-
23 surance; and
24 (f) Building or maintaining horse racing infrastructure that enhances or protects the
25 safety or well-being of racehorses.
26 SECTION 4. ORS 565.446 and 565.447 and section 3 of this 2026 Act are added to and made
