Section 1:
SB 1551-1
(LC 33)
2/6/26 (RLM/ps)
Requested by Senator GELSER BLOUIN
PROPOSED AMENDMENTS TO
1 On page 1 of the printed bill, delete lines 9 through 14 and insert:
2 “(a) The criteria for construction in wildland areas set forth in the
3 International Wildland-Urban Interface Code;
4 “(b) The criteria for construction in wildland areas set forth in the Na-
5 tional Fire Protection Association Standard 1140; or”.
6 Delete lines 26 and 27 and insert:
7 “(a) Prohibits both the removal of materials that are not fire-hardened
8 building materials, as defined in section 2 of this 2026 Act, and the replace-
9 ment of materials that are not fire-hardened building materials with fire-
10 hardened building materials; or”.
11 On page 2, delete lines 5 through 7 and insert:
12 “(B) Requires the use of fire-hardened building materials that cost sub-
13 stantially more than other fire-hardened building materials of similar quality
14 to the materials proposed by the owner such that the cost practically pre-
15 vents the owner from using fire-hardened building materials or imposes an
16 unreasonable burden on the owner.”.
17 In line 11, delete “60” and insert “90”.
18
