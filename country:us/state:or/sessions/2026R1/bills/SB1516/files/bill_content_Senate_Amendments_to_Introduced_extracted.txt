12 “SECTION 3. Definitions. As used in sections 3 to 9 of this 2026 Act:
13 “(1) ‘Alert’ means a notification generated by an automated license plate recognition
14 system indicating that a vehicle’s license plate number or other vehicle characteristics
15 match information contained in a hot list or database described in section 4 of this 2026 Act.
16 “(2) ‘Authorized user’ means a law enforcement officer or other employee of a law
17 enforcement agency who is authorized to deploy or use an automated license plate recogni-
18 tion system or captured license plate data in accordance with the provisions of sections 3 to
19 9 of this 2026 Act.
20 “(3)(a) ‘Automated license plate recognition system’ means one or more high-speed
21 cameras combined with computer algorithms used to convert images of license plates or
22 other vehicle characteristics into computer-readable data.
23 “(b) ‘Automated license plate recognition system’ does not include a photo red light
24 camera described in ORS 810.434 when used for enforcement of ORS 811.265 or a photo radar
25 unit when used for enforcement of ORS 811.111.
26 “(4) ‘Captured license plate data’ means the global positioning system coordinates, date
27 and time, video, photograph, license plate number, vehicle characteristics or any other re-
28 lated data or information collected by an automated license plate recognition system.
29 “(5) ‘Hot list’ means a database created or maintained by a law enforcement agency that
30 identifies vehicles used to facilitate the commission of a crime or violation or to avoid ap-
31 prehension for commission of a crime, or that are associated with a missing or endangered
32 person.
33 “(6) ‘Law enforcement agency’ has the meaning given that term in ORS 181A.775.
34 “(7) ‘Law enforcement purpose’ means an authorized use of an automated license plate
35 recognition system or captured license plate data under section 4 of this 2026 Act.
