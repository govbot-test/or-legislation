CHAPTER .................................................
AN ACT
Relating to professional workforce; creating new provisions; repealing sections 2 and 3, chapter 618,
Oregon Laws 2025; and declaring an emergency.
Be It Enacted by the People of the State of Oregon:
SECTION 1. A professional licensing board as defined in ORS 670.415 shall publish the
guidance described in ORS 670.415 not later than January 1, 2028.
SECTION 2. A professional licensing board as defined in ORS 670.415 shall comply with
the requirements of ORS 670.415 not later than January 1, 2028.
SECTION 3. Sections 2 and 3, chapter 618, Oregon Laws 2025, are repealed.
SECTION 4. This 2026 Act being necessary for the immediate preservation of the public
peace, health and safety, an emergency is declared to exist, and this 2026 Act takes effect
on its passage.
