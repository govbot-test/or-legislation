Section 1:
SENATE AMENDMENTS TO
SENATE BILL 1525
By COMMITTEE ON ENERGY AND ENVIRONMENT
February 18
1 On page 1 of the printed bill, delete lines 8 through 20 and insert:
2 “(2) The task force consists of no fewer than 13 members appointed as follows:
3 “(a)(A) The President of the Senate shall appoint one member from among the members of the
4 Senate.
5 “(B) The Speaker of the House of Representatives shall appoint one member from among mem-
6 bers of the House of Representatives.
7 “(C) Of the members appointed under this paragraph, at least one shall be a member of the
8 coastal caucus.
9 “(b) The President and the Speaker shall jointly appoint not fewer than 10 members to represent:
10 “(A) Public and private research institutions;
11 “(B) Businesses innovating in blue economy sectors;
12 “(C) Commercial and noncommercial fishery businesses;
13 “(D) Recreational activities other than fishing;
14 “(E) The tourism industry;
15 “(F) Environmental organizations;
16 “(G) Equity investors in blue economy businesses;
17 “(H) Coastal labor;
18 “(I) The Sea Grant College of Oregon State University; and
19 “(J) Federally recognized Indian tribes located on the Oregon coast.
20 “(c) The chair of the Ocean Policy Advisory Council, or the chair’s designee, shall serve as a
21 member of the task force.”.
22 On page 2, delete lines 26 through 28 and insert:
23 “(10) The task force shall submit a report and may include recommendations for legislation to
24 the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to the environment, in the manner pro-
25 vided by ORS 192.245, and to the Ocean Policy Advisory Council, no later than September 15,
26 2027.”.
27
