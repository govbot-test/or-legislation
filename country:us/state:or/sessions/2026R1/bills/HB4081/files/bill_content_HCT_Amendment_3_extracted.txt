8 “SECTION 3. Section 4 of this 2026 Act is added to and made a part
9 of the Oregon Vehicle Code.
10 “SECTION 4. (1) Notwithstanding any other provision of law, all
11 amounts received from citations issued under ORS 810.442 shall be
12 used first for the costs of the highway worker photo radar program
13 under ORS 810.441. Any revenue generated that exceeds program costs
14 shall be used for traffic safety purposes as determined by the Depart-
15 ment of Transportation in consultation with the Oregon State Police,
16 which shall include but not be limited to costs associated with speed
17 enforcement in highway work zones.
18 “(2) As used in this section, ‘highway work zone’ has the meaning
19 given that term in ORS 811.230.”.
