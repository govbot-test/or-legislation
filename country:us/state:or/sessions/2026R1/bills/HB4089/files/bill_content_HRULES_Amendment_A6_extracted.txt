Section 1:
HB 4089-A6
(LC 150)
2/19/26 (JLM/ps)
Requested by Representative TRAN
PROPOSED AMENDMENTS TO
1 On page 4 of the printed A-engrossed bill, delete lines 11 through 20 and
2 insert:
3 “(7)(a) A direct contractor or subcontractor commits a Class A
4 misdemeanor if the direct contractor or subcontractor knowingly enters into
5 a contract with a construction labor contractor who is required to be li-
6 censed under ORS 658.405 to 658.511 and who is not properly licensed under
7 ORS 658.405 to 658.511.
8 “(b) A direct contractor or subcontractor commits a Class C felony if the
9 direct contractor or subcontractor knowingly enters into a contract with a
10 construction labor contractor who is required to be licensed under ORS
11 658.405 to 658.511 and who is not properly licensed under ORS 658.405 to
12 658.511, and the direct contractor or subcontractor has a prior conviction
13 under this subsection at the time of the offense.
14 “(c) As used in this subsection:
15 “(A) ‘Construction labor contractor’ has the meaning given that term in
16 ORS 658.405, but does not include a person who is licensed under ORS
17 chapter 701.
18 “(B) ‘Direct contractor’ has the meaning given that term in ORS 652.197.
19 “(C) ‘Subcontractor’ has the meaning given that term in ORS 652.197.”.
