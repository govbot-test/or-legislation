HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
HOUSE BILL 4152
By COMMITTEE ON EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT AND VETERANS
February 11

2 “(2) If one of the parties to an application for a marriage license is unable to submit the appli-
3 cation to the county clerk in person by reason of active service:
