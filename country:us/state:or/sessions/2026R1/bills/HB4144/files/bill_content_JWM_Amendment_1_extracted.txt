Section 1:
HB 4144-1
(LC 27)
2/19/26 (STN/ps)
Requested by JOINT COMMITTEE ON WAYS AND MEANS
PROPOSED AMENDMENTS TO
1 On page 14 of the printed bill, after line 27, insert:
2 “SECTION 23. Notwithstanding any other law limiting expenditures,
3 the limitation on expenditures established by section 2 (3), chapter 426,
4 Oregon Laws 2025, for the biennium ending June 30, 2027, as the max-
5 imum limit for payment of expenses from fees, moneys or other reve-
6 nues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, the proceeds of bonds for the
7 Orphan Site Account and federal funds from congestion mitigation and
8 air quality grants, drinking water protection, laboratory accreditation
9 and woodstove grants and for smoke monitoring laboratory services,
10 but excluding lottery funds and federal funds not described in section
11 2, chapter 426, Oregon Laws 2025, collected or received by the Depart-
12 ment of Environmental Quality, for land quality, is increased by
13 $142,317, to carry out the provisions of this 2026 Act.”.
14 In line 31, delete “23” and insert “24”.
