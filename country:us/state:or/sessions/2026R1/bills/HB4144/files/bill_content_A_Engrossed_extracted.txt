SUMMARY
The following summary is not prepared by the sponsors of the measure and is not a part of the body thereof subject
to consideration by the Legislative Assembly. It is an editor’s brief statement of the essential features of the
measure. The statement includes a measure digest written in compliance with applicable readability standards.
Digest: This Act says that makers of batteries must carry out a plan to collect and recycle
batteries. (Flesch Readability Score: 60.1).
Requires producers of batteries or battery-containing products to join a battery producer re-
sponsibility organization and implement a battery producer responsibility program for the collection
and recycling of batteries.
Directs the Department of Environmental Quality to administer and enforce requirements of the
Act.
Establishes the Battery Producer Responsibility Fund.
Imposes civil penalties for violations of the Act.

24 SECTION 8. Responsible management. (1) A battery producer responsibility organization
25 shall, in compliance with all applicable laws, ensure that the organization’s battery producer
26 responsibility program uses responsible management in collecting, transporting, processing,
27 recycling or otherwise managing covered batteries and materials from covered batteries.
28 Responsible management includes:
29 (a) Adequate record keeping;
30 (b) Tracking the fate of covered batteries;
31 (c) Conducting performance audits and inspections;
32 (d) Complying with worker health and safety requirements;
33 (e) Ensuring that materials from covered batteries are delivered to responsible end
34 markets;
35 (f) Maintaining liability insurance and other financial assurances; and
36 (g) Carrying out other practices related to the duties of a battery producer responsibility
37 organization, as may be adopted by rule by the Environmental Quality Commission.
38 (2) A battery producer responsibility organization shall ensure that all collection sites
39 and processors that manage covered batteries or materials from covered batteries collected
40 through the battery producer responsibility program use responsible management with re-
41 spect to covered batteries and materials from covered batteries.

SUMMARY
The following summary is not prepared by the sponsors of the measure and is not a part of the body thereof subject
to consideration by the Legislative Assembly. It is an editor’s brief statement of the essential features of the
measure. The statement includes a measure digest written in compliance with applicable readability standards.
Digest: This Act says that makers of batteries must carry out a plan to collect and recycle
batteries. (Flesch Readability Score: 60.1).
Requires producers of batteries or battery-containing products to join a battery producer re-
sponsibility organization and implement a battery producer responsibility program for the collection
and recycling of batteries.
Directs the Department of Environmental Quality to administer and enforce requirements of the
Act.
Establishes the Battery Producer Responsibility Fund.
Imposes civil penalties for violations of the Act.
