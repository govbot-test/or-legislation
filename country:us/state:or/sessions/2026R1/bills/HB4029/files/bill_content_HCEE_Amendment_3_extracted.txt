PROPOSED AMENDMENTS TO
1 On page 1 of the printed bill, line 21, after the second “electrician,” insert
2 “a limited residential electrician,”.
3 On page 7, after line 11, insert:
4 “(6) If a solar energy contractor or the person that installs a solar energy
5 system provides a warranty under subsection (2)(m) of this section, the war-
6 ranty must transfer to a purchaser of the residential real property to which
7 the warranty applies and remain in effect for the duration of the warranty
8 term.”.
9 On page 11, after line 2, insert:
10 “(4) If a solar energy contractor or the person that installs a solar energy
11 system provides a warranty for repairs to a resident’s property as a conse-
12 quence of the installation or removal of the solar energy system, the war-
13 ranty must transfer to a purchaser of the residential real property to which
14 the warranty applies and remain in effect for the duration of the warranty
15 term.”.
