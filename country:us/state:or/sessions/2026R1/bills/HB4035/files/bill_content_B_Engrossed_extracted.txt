SUMMARY
The following summary is not prepared by the sponsors of the measure and is not a part of the body thereof subject
to consideration by the Legislative Assembly. It is an editor’s brief statement of the essential features of the
measure. The statement includes a measure digest written in compliance with applicable readability standards.
Digest: This Act amends temp UGB program and lets LCDC award grants. (Flesch Readability
Score: 80.3).
Expands eligibility for cities and Metro to amend their urban growth boundaries under a tem-
porary program.
Provides an alternative pathway within the temporary program by which the City of
Woodburn may amend its urban growth boundary.
Authorizes the Land Conservation and Development Commission to issue grants to implement
the commission’s duties.
Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
