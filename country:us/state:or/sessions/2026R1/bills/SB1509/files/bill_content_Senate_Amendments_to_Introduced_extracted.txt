Section 1:
SENATE AMENDMENTS TO
SENATE BILL 1509
By COMMITTEE ON RULES
February 17
1 On page 1 of the printed bill, delete lines 15 and 16 and insert:
2 “(c) If the candidates for President and Vice President are not affiliated with a political party,
3 registered as not being affiliated with any political party.”.
4 In line 17, delete “an individual who is selected as”.
5 In line 18, restore “candidate” and delete “individual”.
6 In line 19, delete “winning”.
7 In line 20, before the comma insert “of the political party, if any, that nominated or appointed
8 the candidate for elector”.
9 On page 2, line 24, after “shall” insert “immediately”.
10 Delete lines 35 through 41 and insert:
11 “(6)(a) Each elector shall complete two ballots, one for President and one for Vice President.
12 The elector shall cast a vote for the candidates for President and Vice President of the political
13 party, if any, that nominated or appointed the elector, except as provided under paragraph (b) of this
14 subsection.
15 “(b) If the candidate for President or Vice President dies or withdraws after the date of the
16 election but before the meeting of electors, each elector shall vote for the replacement candidate for
17 President or Vice President who has been selected in accordance with the rules and bylaws of the
18 political party, if any.”.
19 On page 3, delete lines 5 and 6 and insert:
20 “(c) Following the examination under paragraph (a) of this subsection, if a declaration is made
21 under paragraph (b) of this subsection, the vacancy shall be filled in accordance with subsection (4)
22 of this section. Prior to the voice vote to”.
23 In line 8, after “shall” insert “immediately”.
24
