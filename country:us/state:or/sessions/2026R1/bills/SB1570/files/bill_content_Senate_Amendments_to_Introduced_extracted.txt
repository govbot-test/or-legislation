SENATE AMENDMENTS TO

By COMMITTEE ON HEALTH CARE
February 20

3 “SECTION 1. (1) As used in this section:
4 “(a) ‘Hospital’ has the meaning given that term in ORS 442.015.
5 “(b) ‘Individually identifiable health information’ has the meaning given that term in ORS
6 192.556.
7 “(c) ‘Information concerning a person’s citizenship or immigration status’ has the
8 meaning given that term in ORS 180.805.
9 “(d) ‘Law enforcement authority’ includes a federal, state and local law enforcement au-
10 thority.
11 “(2) A hospital shall designate which areas of the hospital are not open to the
12 public. Areas designated as not open to the public must include any area:
13 “(a) Where entry or access to the area is controlled; or
14 “(b) That is a patient room.
15 “(3) A hospital may not disclose for the purpose of law enforcement individually identifi-
16 able health information or information concerning a person’s citizenship or immigration
17 status unless disclosure is:
18 “(a) Required by state or federal law or an order of a court of competent jurisdiction,
19 including a judicial warrant; or
20 “(b) For the purpose of ensuring continuity of care.
21 “(4) A hospital shall adopt and maintain written policies and procedures that:
22 “(a) Address how the hospital will respond if a law enforcement authority arrives at the
23 hospital; and
24 “(b) Identify for the hospital at least one administrator in a supervisory role who is
25 designated to respond when a law enforcement authority arrives at the hospital.
26 “SECTION 2. (1) As used in this section, ‘health care facility’ means:
27 “(a) A hospital as defined in ORS 442.015.
28 “(b) A federally qualified health center as defined in 42 U.S.C. 1396d(l)(2).
29 “(2) It is an unlawful employment practice for a health care facility to retaliate or take
30 disciplinary action against an employee of the health care facility because the employee has
31 distributed informational or educational materials concerning immigrant rights and available
32 immigration legal services if the materials are published or otherwise made available by a
33 state agency.
34 “(3) An employee who alleges a violation of this section may file a complaint with the
35 Commissioner of the Bureau of Labor and Industries in the manner provided by ORS
LC 290/SB 1570-5

6 “SECTION 4. (1) As used in this section:
7 “(a) ‘Health care facility’ means:
8 “(A) A hospital as defined in ORS 442.015.
