Section 1:
HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
A-ENGROSSED SENATE BILL 1570
By COMMITTEE ON RULES
February 26
1 On page 1 of the printed A-engrossed bill, delete lines 4 through 15.
2 On page 2, delete lines 1 through 11 and insert:
3 “SECTION 1. (1) As used in this section:
4 “(a) ‘Hospital’ has the meaning given that term in ORS 442.015.
5 “(b)(A) ‘Law enforcement authority’ includes a federal, state or local law enforcement
6 authority.
7 “(B) ‘Law enforcement authority’ does not include a police department established under
8 ORS 353.125.
9 “(2) A hospital shall adopt and maintain written policies and procedures that:
10 “(a) Address how the hospital will respond if a law enforcement authority arrives at the
11 hospital; and
12 “(b) Identify for the hospital at least one administrator in a supervisory role who is
13 designated to respond when a law enforcement authority arrives at the hospital.
14 “(3) For purposes of responding to a law enforcement authority who arrives at the hos-
15 pital, a hospital shall designate which areas of the hospital are not open to the public. Areas
16 designated as not open to the public must include any area:
17 “(a) Where entry or access to the area is controlled; or
18 “(b) That is a patient room.”.
19 In line 33, after “(2)” insert “Except as provided in subsection (3) of this section,”.
20 After line 36, insert:
21 “(3) Notwithstanding ORS 192.558 (2)(b), a health care facility may not disclose for the purpose
22 of law enforcement information concerning a person’s citizenship or immigration status or a person’s
23 country of birth unless disclosure is required by state or federal law or an order of a court of
24 competent jurisdiction.”.
25
