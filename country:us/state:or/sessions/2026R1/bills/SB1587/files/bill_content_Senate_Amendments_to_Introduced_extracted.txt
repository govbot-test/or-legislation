Section 1:
SENATE AMENDMENTS TO
SENATE BILL 1587
By COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY
February 19
1 Delete lines 4 through 25 of the printed bill and insert:
2 “SECTION 1. (1) As used in this section:
3 “(a) ‘Data broker’ has the meaning given that term in ORS 646A.593.
4 “(b) ‘Personally identifiable information’ has the meaning given that term in ORS 432.005.
5 “(c) ‘Public body’ has the meaning given that term in ORS 174.109.
6 “(2)(a) Except as provided in subsection (3) of this section, a public body may not disclose
7 personally identifiable information to a data broker unless the data broker first provides a
8 written attestation to the public body that the information will not be sold or otherwise
9 transferred to any entity that will use the information to enforce federal immigration law.
10 “(b) If a public body reasonably believes that a data broker’s written attestation contains
11 material misrepresentations, falsehoods or omissions, the public body shall reject the written
12 attestation and shall decline to disclose personally identifiable information to the data bro-
13 ker.
14 “(3) This section does not prohibit a public body from disclosing personally identifiable
15 information to a data broker if the disclosure is required under ORS 192.311 to 192.478 or
16 required by an order of a court of competent jurisdiction.
17 “SECTION 2. This 2026 Act being necessary for the immediate preservation of the public
18 peace, health and safety, an emergency is declared to exist, and this 2026 Act takes effect
19 on its passage.”.
