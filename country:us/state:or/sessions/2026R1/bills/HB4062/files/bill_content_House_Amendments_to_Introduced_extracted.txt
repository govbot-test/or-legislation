HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
HOUSE BILL 4062
By JOINT COMMITTEE ON WAYS AND MEANS
February 27
1 After line 19 of the printed bill, insert:
2 “SECTION 2. Notwithstanding any other law limiting expenditures, the limitation on
3 expenditures established by section 11 (1), chapter 616, Oregon Laws 2025, for the biennium
4 ending June 30, 2027, as the maximum limit for payment of expenses from lottery moneys
5 allocated from the Administrative Services Economic Development Fund to the Oregon
6 Business Development Department, is increased by $350,000 for expenses related to imple-
7 mentation of section 1 of this 2026 Act.”.
