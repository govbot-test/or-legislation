Section 1:
HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
HOUSE BILL 4143
By COMMITTEE ON RULES
February 16
1 On page 1 of the printed bill, delete lines 11 through 13 and insert:
2 “(c) ‘Qualified payment’ means moneys that a state agency is obligated to transfer to the federal
3 government pursuant to contract or federal law, but excluding:
4 “(A) Payroll taxes for officers or employees of state agencies;
5 “(B) Amounts voluntarily withheld from unemployment insurance and paid family and medical
6 leave insurance benefits for federal income tax purposes; and
7 “(C) Any revenues collected or overpaid benefits recovered pursuant to the Federal Unemploy-
8 ment Tax Act or ORS chapter 657.”.
9 On page 2, after line 17, insert:
10 “SECTION 3. (1) Sections 1 and 2 of this 2026 Act are repealed on January 2, 2037.
11 “(2) Any moneys remaining in the Federal Moneys Holding Account established under
12 section 2 of this 2026 Act on the date specified in subsection (1) of this section shall be
13 transferred to the General Fund to be used for general governmental purposes.”.
14 In line 18, delete “3” and insert “4”.
15
