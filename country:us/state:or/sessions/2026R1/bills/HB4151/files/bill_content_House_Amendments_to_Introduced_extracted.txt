Section 1:
HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
HOUSE BILL 4151
By COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY
February 18
1 On page 1 of the printed bill, delete lines 9 through 12 and insert:
2 “(b) Notwithstanding paragraph (a) of this subsection, initiating a false report is a Class C fel-
3 ony if the false report is of such nature that the person initiating the report intentionally causes the
4 deployment of an enhanced law enforcement response and recklessly causes serious physical injury
5 to or the death of another person as a result of the enhanced law enforcement response.”.
6 Delete lines 25 through 28 and insert:
7 “(5) As used in this section, ‘enhanced law enforcement response’ means a response by a law
8 enforcement agency that, based on the information received, involves the dispatch of two or more
9 officers or units and one or more of the following:
10 “(a) The dispatch of specialized or tactical teams;”.
11
