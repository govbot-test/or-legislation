Section 1:
HB 4127-3
(LC 179)
2/9/26 (RH/ps)
Requested by Representative BOWMAN
PROPOSED AMENDMENTS TO
1 On page 2 of the printed bill, delete line 14 and insert:
2 “(b) ‘Prohibited entity’ means a nonprofit reproductive health care pro-
3 vider that:
4 “(A) Received more than $800,000 in Medicaid reimbursements in 2023;
5 and
6 “(B) Is not eligible to receive federal Medicaid funds.”.
7 Delete lines 21 through 27 and insert:
8 “SECTION 7. (1) Section 6 of this 2026 Act becomes operative only
9 if a state or federal law, rule, regulation or other government action
10 is enacted, adopted or taken that prohibits a prohibited entity, as de-
11 fined in section 2 of this 2026 Act, from maintaining enrollment in the
12 state medical assistance program.
13 “(2) If a state or federal law, rule, regulation or other government
14 action described in subsection (1) of this section is enacted, adopted
15 or taken, section 6 of this 2026 Act becomes operative on the effective
16 date of that law, rule, regulation or other government action.
17 “(3) The Oregon Health Authority shall immediately notify the
18 Legislative Counsel if a state or federal law, rule, regulation or other
19 government action described in subsection (1) of this section is en-
20 acted, adopted or taken.”.
