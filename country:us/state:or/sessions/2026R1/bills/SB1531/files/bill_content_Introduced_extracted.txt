1 A BILL FOR AN ACT
2 Relating to funding for emergency medical services.
3 Be It Enacted by the People of the State of Oregon:
4 SECTION 1. (1) The Oregon Health Authority shall study the feasibility of funding
5 emergency medical services through a universal health care model that would include:
6 (a) A mix of public and private resources;
7 (b) State funding and oversight;
8 (c) Allocation of funding to counties and county-level oversight; and
9 (d) Delegated local control.

11 include recommendations for legislation, to the interim committees of the Legislative As-
12 sembly related to emergency management no later than September 15, 2027.
