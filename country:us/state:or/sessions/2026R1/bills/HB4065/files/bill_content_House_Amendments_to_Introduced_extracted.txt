Section 1:
HOUSE AMENDMENTS TO
HOUSE BILL 4065
By COMMITTEE ON AGRICULTURE, LAND USE, NATURAL RESOURCES, AND
WATER
February 13
1 On page 1 of the printed bill, delete lines 4 through 29 and delete pages 2 and 3 and insert:
2 “SECTION 1. ORS 576.738 is amended to read:
3 “576.738. (1) If a seed dealer fails to pay a producer for agricultural seed when payment is due
4 under a seed production contract or fails to pay a seed grower for grass seed when payment is due
5 under a seed purchase contract, the producer or seed grower may notify the State Department of
6 Agriculture. Upon notification by a producer or seed grower, the department shall determine
7 whether payment has been made when due. If the department determines that the seed dealer has
8 not made a payment that is due under a seed production contract or seed purchase contract, the
9 department shall notify the seed dealer in writing [that the] of the following requirements:
10 “(a) The seed dealer [has 30 days to] must pay the producer or seed grower all delinquent
11 amounts plus interest on each delinquent amount. Interest shall be calculated at the rate of one
12 percent above the bank prime loan interest rate set forth in the most recent Federal Reserve
13 Statistical Release H.15 per month [simple interest] from the final payment date for that delinquent
14 amount.
15 “(b) The payment required under paragraph (a) of this subsection must occur within 30
16 days of the date that the department’s written notice was issued to the seed dealer.
17 “(c) Within the 30-day period specified in paragraph (b) of this subsection, the seed dealer
18 must provide to the department a written notice and supporting documentation verifying
19 that the seed dealer has made the payment required under paragraph (a) of this subsection.
20 “(2) A seed production contract or seed purchase contract may not vary the terms of the remedy
21 provided by this section. [A seed dealer may appeal the notice] Notice given by the department under
22 this section [as provided in] is subject to ORS chapter 183. This section does not prevent a pro-
23 ducer or seed grower from filing a notice of lien against a seed dealer.
24 “(3) If a seed dealer fails to make payment [as required by a notice given by the department under
25 this section] within the 30-day period specified in subsection (1) of this section, the department
26 shall suspend any seed dealer license issued to the dealer until the dealer demonstrates to the sat-
27 isfaction of the department that the dealer is current on all payments due to all producers and seed
28 growers. An order suspending a license under this subsection is subject to ORS chapter 183. How-
29 ever, the department may not suspend a seed dealer license under this section using the procedure
30 described in ORS 183.430 (2).
31 “(4) A seed dealer that fails to make payment on a seed production contract or seed purchase
32 contract as required by a notice given by the department under this section is considered to have
33 authorized the producer or seed grower to sell in a commercially reasonable manner any seed from
34 the contract that is still in the possession of the producer or seed grower. This subsection does not
35 prevent a seed dealer from giving consent to the producer or seed grower by other means and does
LC 89/HB 4065-1
1 not supersede the terms of a consent given by other means.
2 “(5) The department may charge a producer or seed grower a fee, not to exceed $100 [$50], for
3 determining whether payment has been made in accordance with the terms of a seed production
4 contract or seed purchase contract. The department may charge a producer or seed grower a fee,
5 not to exceed $400 [$200], for notifying a seed dealer in writing regarding the deadline for payment
6 of delinquent amounts plus interest.
7 “SECTION 2. ORS 576.802 is amended to read:
