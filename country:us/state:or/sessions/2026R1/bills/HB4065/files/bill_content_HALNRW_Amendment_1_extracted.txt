4 “576.738. (1) If a seed dealer fails to pay a producer for agricultural seed
5 when payment is due under a seed production contract or fails to pay a seed
6 grower for grass seed when payment is due under a seed purchase contract,
7 the producer or seed grower may notify the State Department of Agriculture.
8 Upon notification by a producer or seed grower, the department shall deter-
9 mine whether payment has been made when due. If the department deter-
10 mines that the seed dealer has not made a payment that is due under a seed
11 production contract or seed purchase contract, the department shall notify
12 the seed dealer in writing [that the] of the following requirements:
13 “(a) The seed dealer [has 30 days to] must pay the producer or seed
14 grower all delinquent amounts plus interest on each delinquent amount.
15 Interest shall be calculated at the rate of one percent above the bank
16 prime loan interest rate set forth in the most recent Federal Reserve
17 Statistical Release H.15 per month [simple interest] from the final payment
18 date for that delinquent amount.
19 “(b) The payment required under paragraph (a) of this subsection
20 must occur within 30 days of the date that the department’s written
21 notice was issued to the seed dealer.

1 “(c) Within the 30-day period specified in paragraph (b) of this
2 subsection, the seed dealer must provide to the department a written
3 notice and supporting documentation verifying that the seed dealer
4 has made the payment required under paragraph (a) of this subsection.
5 “(2) A seed production contract or seed purchase contract may not vary
6 the terms of the remedy provided by this section. [A seed dealer may appeal
7 the notice] Notice given by the department under this section [as provided
8 in] is subject to ORS chapter 183. This section does not prevent a producer
9 or seed grower from filing a notice of lien against a seed dealer.
10 “(3) If a seed dealer fails to make payment [as required by a notice given
11 by the department under this section] within the 30-day period specified in
12 subsection (1) of this section, the department shall suspend any seed dealer
13 license issued to the dealer until the dealer demonstrates to the satisfaction
14 of the department that the dealer is current on all payments due to all pro-
15 ducers and seed growers. An order suspending a license under this subsection
16 is subject to ORS chapter 183. However, the department may not suspend a
17 seed dealer license under this section using the procedure described in ORS
18 183.430 (2).
19 “(4) A seed dealer that fails to make payment on a seed production con-
20 tract or seed purchase contract as required by a notice given by the depart-

24 subsection does not prevent a seed dealer from giving consent to the pro-
