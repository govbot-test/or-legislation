Section 1:
A-Engrossed
House Bill 4065
Ordered by the House February 13
Including House Amendments dated February 13
Sponsored by Representative SCHARF; Representative LEVY B (Presession filed.)
SUMMARY
The following summary is not prepared by the sponsors of the measure and is not a part of the body thereof subject
to consideration by the Legislative Assembly. It is an editor’s brief statement of the essential features of the
measure. The statement includes a measure digest written in compliance with applicable readability standards.
Digest: The Act makes changes related to the late payment of seed contracts. The Act declares
an emergency, effective on passage. (Flesch Readability Score: 61.3).
Modifies provisions relating to late payment of seed production contracts and seed purchase
contracts for agricultural seed and other seed.
Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT
2 Relating to seed contract payments; amending ORS 576.738 and 576.802; and declaring an emergency.
3 Be It Enacted by the People of the State of Oregon:
4 SECTION 1. ORS 576.738 is amended to read:
5 576.738. (1) If a seed dealer fails to pay a producer for agricultural seed when payment is due
6 under a seed production contract or fails to pay a seed grower for grass seed when payment is due
7 under a seed purchase contract, the producer or seed grower may notify the State Department of
8 Agriculture. Upon notification by a producer or seed grower, the department shall determine
9 whether payment has been made when due. If the department determines that the seed dealer has
10 not made a payment that is due under a seed production contract or seed purchase contract, the
11 department shall notify the seed dealer in writing [that the] of the following requirements:
12 (a) The seed dealer [has 30 days to] must pay the producer or seed grower all delinquent
13 amounts plus interest on each delinquent amount. Interest shall be calculated at the rate of one
14 percent above the bank prime loan interest rate set forth in the most recent Federal Reserve
15 Statistical Release H.15 per month [simple interest] from the final payment date for that delinquent
16 amount.
17 (b) The payment required under paragraph (a) of this subsection must occur within 30
18 days of the date that the department’s written notice was issued to the seed dealer.
19 (c) Within the 30-day period specified in paragraph (b) of this subsection, the seed dealer
20 must provide to the department a written notice and supporting documentation verifying
21 that the seed dealer has made the payment required under paragraph (a) of this subsection.
22 (2) A seed production contract or seed purchase contract may not vary the terms of the remedy
23 provided by this section. [A seed dealer may appeal the notice] Notice given by the department under
24 this section [as provided in] is subject to ORS chapter 183. This section does not prevent a producer
25 or seed grower from filing a notice of lien against a seed dealer.
26 (3) If a seed dealer fails to make payment [as required by a notice given by the department under
